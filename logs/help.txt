MIGRATION STAGES
----------------
'exported' - The VM has been exported and the OVA is ready for import.
'imported' - The VM has been imported into CloudPlatform and the upload process has been kicked off.
'launched' - Transition state after the VM is launched in CloudPlatform and before the migration has been cleaned up.
'migrated' - The VM has been successfully migrated to CloudPlatform and is up and running.
